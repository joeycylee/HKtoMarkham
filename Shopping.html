Shopping

General rules:

  • Do not buy Canadian food (like cereal) in Chinese supermarkets. It's more expensive! Buy Canadian food in Canadian grocery stores.

There are different levels of Canadian grocery stores:

  1. Best prices, but may have lower quality fruits and vegetables
    • Walmart
    • No Frills
    • Food Basics
    • FreshCo
    • Dollarama = the dollar store. They sell snacks, home items, decorations, etc.
  2. Okay prices
    • Metro
    • Loblaws
    • T&T
    • Costco
    • Longo's
    • Real Canadian Superstore
  3. Expensive
    • Sobeys
    • Whole Foods
    • Fortinos
    • Farm Boy - good for special good quality food, like fresh pies, homemade fries, fresh soup, etc.
    • Rabba

Many stores have their own points programs:

  • PC Optimum - You can collect and use PC points at Shoppers Drug Mart (pharmacy), Loblaws (food), No Frills (food) and Esso (gasoline)
  • Scene+ - You can collect and use Scene points at Cineplex (movies), Sobeys (food), FreshCo (food), and Foodland (food)

Canadian recommendations:

  • Winners, Marshalls, and Home Sense
    • These stores sell new clothes (including brand name) for cheaper
    • Winners and Marshalls mostly sell clothes and some home items
    • Home Sense sells furniture, kitchen items, etc.
  • Value Village sells second hand clothes and home items
  • Plato's Closet also sells second hand clothes. You can sell your clothes there too, but they have to be good quality and in style.

When do sales happen?

  • August - September: back to school season
  • Black Friday: November 24, 2023
  • Boxing Day: December 26
  • Chocolate is usually on sale the day after Halloween and Valentine's Day
