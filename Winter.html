Winter

  • Wear layers. Snow pants are recommended if you will be spending a lot of time in the snow (skiing, making a snowman).
  • Get good winter boots so you don't slip. This website tells you how good your boots are.
  • Scarves are very useful.
  • Wipe salt off your clothes and shoes as soon as you can. Salt will eat into the fabric and ruin your clothes.
  • Wear thick gloves and socks if outside for a long time. It is possible to lose your fingers and toes to frost bite.
  • Don't throw snowballs with ice at people's faces.
  • Before a snow storm, put some salt on your porch and driveway so it does not get too slippery in the morning.
  • Fun things to make with snow: tunnels, snow forts, snowmen, snow angels, a chair
